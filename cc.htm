Concordance

This concordance only contains words that are at least two letters long. This concordance is case-sensitive, so that ‘THE’ is treated as a different word from ‘the’.
Words appearing 50 times or less are included within the index files for each alphabet themselves. Other words appearing 1000 times or less are included in their own files. Any words occuring more than 1000 times in the Bible will only show the word itself with the number of occurrences, but will not list out all the verse references.
